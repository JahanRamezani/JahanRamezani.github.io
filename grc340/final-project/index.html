Hi, I'm Jahan Ramezani. My enemies call me Jonkers.

If there's one thing I'm pretentious about, it's shoving my weird music taste in the face of people who don't care. That's why, for my GrC 340 Final Project, I've made an interactive Vinyl shelf that you can decorate! The catch? It's all MY music. If you like psych rock, folk, or anything else weird, I think you'll enjoy this. Don't? Uh, then I'm sorry you had to deal with this.

jQuery was quite hard to use, especially since I had to learn a bit about jQuery UI for this project. I think I bit off a bit more than I could chew. So I wanted to credit Tara Jensen for her jQuery UI Drag and Sort template, which I used as a starting point for this project. Check out the Codepen File here

HOW TO PLAY

I should preface that this has a few bugs. I think this was way above my paygrade. First, scrol down and enter your name! Js will store that data, and show it on the next page! Once you enter the game, feel free to drag the Vinyl's in the bottom left corner around the screen! You can put it into the record player and enjoy a song off the album. You can also put them vinyls on your shelf, and it will prompt you to enter the artist name and genre. If you get it correct, the data will store! You can click on a vinyl again to see what Genre and Artist it is. Need a hint? The album credits are on the End tab!
